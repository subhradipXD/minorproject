Help Desk

Welcome to our platform! Below, you'll find helpful information on how to use our website.

Getting Started

  1. Registering an Account: To access all features, you need to register an account. Click on the "Register" or "Sign Up" link and follow the instructions.
  2. Logging In: Once registered, you can log in using your credentials on the "Login" page.

Using the Platform

  1. Navigation: Use the navigation menu to explore different sections of the website, such as events, research works, and institutions.
  2. Searching: Use the search bar to find specific information. Enter keywords related to events, research works, or institutions.

Uploading Content

  1. Event Upload: If you're a faculty member, use the "Upload Event" feature to share information about events organized by your institution.
  2. Research Work Upload: Faculty can also upload details about research works using the "Upload Research Work" feature.

Contact Us

If you have specific questions or encounter issues not addressed here, feel free to reach out to us through the Contact Us page.
